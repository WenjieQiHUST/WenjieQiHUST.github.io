Brief Biography: My name is Wenjie Qi (亓文杰). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science form Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2025. Before that, I received my B.Sc. in Electronic Information Science from Changsha University of Science and Technology (CSUST).

Now I am a Senior Software Engineer in XIAOMI and study storage/filesystems on HyperOS. I am honored to be one of the XIAOMI FutureStars.
